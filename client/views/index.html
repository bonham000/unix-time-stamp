This service allows you to make a request to a server and view the corresponding natural and Unix time as output.

The example input: https://unix-time-stamp.herokuapp.com/September 06 1999

returns this object: {"unix":"936576000","natural":"September 06, 1999"}

You may submit a Unix timestamp or natural date, according to these guidelines:

  • Natural date queries must be formatted: Month-DD-YYYY
  • The month may be abbreviated to the first three letters, e.g. "Sep", or written in full, e.g. "September"
  • Your query may include or exclude spaces

Example Queries:

  • Valid: "Sep 04 2016"
  • Valid: "March291999"
  • Valid: "Aug 01 2090"
  • Invalid: "Sep 4 2016"
  • Invalid: "Sept 4 2016"
  • Invalid: "Jan -1 1970"

Invalid queries will return "null" for both properties.
